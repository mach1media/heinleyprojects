Meet Brian

Brian Boyce has been in the piano bar scene for nearly a decade and has traveled all over the state of Texas to play shows and events and has also traveled to Louisiana, Arkansas, and Oklahoma!

With a Bachelors of Fine Arts in Musical Theatre from the University of Texas Arlington, he has used his love of music and performance to make a career. He has been a full time musician playing since he was 19 years old and has performed in a wide spectrum of venues, including leading worship at his home church in Austin, performing at Six Flags over Texas in Arlington, leading and working with several wedding bands, and of course, working with multiple piano bar companies.

While he has worked in such a wide variety of venues including 10 years in piano bars, Brian has used his training to develop a wide repertoire to play a vast pool of song requests!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dueling Pianos?

Dueling Pianos or ‘Sing-along’ pianos are a form of entertainment with two pianists, usually facing each other, who sing together and take turns playing popular music. It is often a song request based show.

What can you expect from a Bb Dueling Pianos show?

Bb Dueling Pianos is our ‘iteration’ of the sing-along piano show. It is built around crowd participation, comedy that can be family friendly or raunchy bar room humor, and an amazing sing-along show that can be anything from Metallica to Billy Joel! Our musicians are veterans who have played for years in the piano bar scene as well as accomplished musicians in their own right.

Is Bb Dueling Pianos right for my event?

As much as we would love to say yes, sometimes an interactive, comedic, crowd participation type of show might not be the right idea for some of you. We’re always looking to add to our portfolio so if you’re wondering if dueling pianos is right for your event, feel free to ask.

What kind of events do we do?

We love to try to play as many different events as we can! Such as fundraisers, weddings, private events, corporate parties, bar events/openings, and even birthday parties! So if you don’t see your event listed here, email us at bbduelingpianos@gmail.com. We’d love to answer your questions!
